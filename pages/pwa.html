PWA page

An installable Progressive Web App (PWA) responsive website that looks good on desktop, tablet, or mobile.

This is the SimplePWA addition stuff.

A Progressive Web App (PWA) is a great thing. On something like your phone, it looks, feels, and acts like a regular phone app. However...

  1. Whereas an app downloaded onto your phone takes up a bunch of your phone's storage space, a PWA takes very little space.
  2. It looks, acts, and feels just like a regular app.
  3. Updates done to the app are instantaneous. There's no need to "update."
  4. You're free from a big corporation's app store and their rules and whims.

The PureDogPWA relies heavily on the SimplePWA.com template. That site links to very helpful tools in setting up proper images for your PWA as well.
